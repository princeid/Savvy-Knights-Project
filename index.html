Chess For Kids

Our goal is to change our children's approach towards learning by making classroom activities more fun.

We seek to integrate chess in as many primary schools in Nigeria as possible to help foster the students' progress in subjects that require critical thinking skills such as quantitative reasoning, mathematics, and general sciences. Our aim extends beyond just classroom activities. Read More...
